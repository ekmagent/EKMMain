# Press Pitch — NJ Medigap Rate Index 2026

**The asset:** https://medicareyourself.com/medicare-supplement/new-jersey/rate-index

**The story (one sentence):** New Jersey seniors pay anywhere from $147.88 to
$260.98 a month for *federally identical* Medicare Supplement Plan G coverage —
a $113/month gap — and most never find out because carriers don't publish
each other's rates.

## Why an editor should care

- Concrete, verifiable, local: filed-rate data (CSG Actuarial), not survey fluff.
- Timely hook: Humana's NJ Plan G block filed +7.01% (June 2024) then +16.22%
  (August 2025), and its NJ loss ratio crossed 100% in 2025 — more increases
  are structurally likely. Rate-increase letters are landing in NJ mailboxes.
- A consumer can act on it the day the story runs: same benefits, pick the
  cheaper filing.
- NJ-specific angles no national outlet covers: community rating (premiums
  don't rise with age here — unlike PA/OH/TX) and the tobacco-neutral
  6-month open enrollment window.

## Rules for the send

- One outlet per email, personalized first line (name a recent relevant story
  they ran). Never BCC-blast.
- Offer the data, not the business: "happy to share the carrier-level table"
  — the broker mention comes naturally in attribution.
- Attribution ask: "MedicareYourself.com, an independent Medicare broker in
  Cherry Hill" + link. The link is the entire SEO/AI payoff.
- Follow up once, 4–5 business days later, two sentences max.
