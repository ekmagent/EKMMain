**TITLE:**
New Jersey Medigap Rates Range From $148 to $261 for Identical Plan G Coverage, New Filed-Rate Index Shows

**SUBHEADING:**
New Jersey Medigap rates for the same federally standardized Plan G vary by $113 per month across 18 carriers, according to a new public rate index published by MedicareYourself, an independent Cherry Hill brokerage.

**BODY:**

New Jersey Medigap rates for Medicare Supplement Plan G run from $147.88 to $260.98 per month for a 65-year-old — a $113 monthly gap for coverage that is identical by federal law — according to the New Jersey Medigap Rate Index published this month by MedicareYourself.com.

The index, built from CSG Actuarial filed-rate data, publishes the numbers carriers file with regulators but do not advertise: the lowest and highest filed rate in the state, the spread between them, household-discount availability, and how New Jersey compares with the lowest filed Plan G rate in Pennsylvania, Ohio, Texas, and North Carolina.

"Every Plan G pays the same claims. The only thing that varies is the premium — and most people never see the whole board," said Anthony Orner, the licensed independent Medicare broker (NPN 20586791) who publishes the index. "A New Jersey retiree who picks the most expensive filing pays about $1,357 more per year than a neighbor with the cheapest one, for the exact same benefits."

The index also tracks rate-increase filings. Humana's New Jersey Plan G block filed a 7.01% increase in 2024 and 16.22% in 2025, while its New Jersey loss ratio — claims paid versus premium collected — crossed 100% in 2025, a pattern that historically precedes further increases.

New Jersey's carrier-by-carrier pricing methods — most NJ carriers use attained-age rating, while a minority are community-rated — and its 6-month Medigap Open Enrollment Period are covered in the index methodology.

The full dataset is free to cite with attribution at medicareyourself.com/medicare-supplement/new-jersey/rate-index. Consumers can compare every carrier's filed rate for their own ZIP code and age by calling 855-559-1700.
